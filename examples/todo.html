Tagr Demo

Tagr modules are self-contained, by design. It's up to the author to properly separate their models, views, and controllers. The upshot of Tagr modules is that you are working entirely in the language of JavaScript: no more maintaining files in various languages, where widget code becomes very distant from each other.
